Returns Policy

Easy and hassle-free returns process

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Please note that certain items are not eligible for returns, including:
  • Software and digital products
  • Personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

1. Package Item

Include all original packaging and accessories

2. Fill Form

Complete the return form included in your package

3. Ship Back

Use the provided return shipping label

4. Get Refund

Receive refund within 5-7 business days

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • In the original packaging with all accessories
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Returned within 14 days of delivery

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  • We will inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  • If approved, your refund will be processed
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation

Important Notes

  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • Refunds exclude original shipping charges
  • Damaged items must be reported within 48 hours
  • Custom items cannot be returned
  • Sale items may have different return policies
